Our Leadership

Sofia Fuentes

Founder

As a first-generation immigrant, Sofia has experienced firsthand the challenges that underserved communities face in accessing basic services, particularly healthcare. These challenges have ignited her passion for social change, motivating her to lead initiatives that promote health equity.

In her role as Founder and President of a university club dedicated to community service and social change, Sofia has developed strong leadership skills, coordinating initiatives that address local health disparities and advocating for marginalized populations. She also collaborates on research projects focused on policy reform and healthcare equity.

Dr. Curtis Preik

Medical Director, Camino Health

Dr. Preik has helped forward our plan to Camino Health's CEO, and runs the clinics being the first mode of contact on what the clients they serve need.

Future Expansion

In our 2-5 year strategic plan, we aim to expand this initiative across the UNC system, beginning with UNC Chapel Hill's MERA program which assists Arabic-speaking refugees. This expansion will create a network of health literacy programs across North Carolina's universities, maximizing our impact on diverse communities.

Two Years

  • Expansion to UNC Chapel Hill
  • Replicable Program Model
  • Securing Long-Term Funding
  • Institutional Integration
  • Policy & Advocacy Efforts
