Our Partnerships

Camino Health Center (Primary Partner)

Camino Health is a bilingual, multicultural clinic that serves underserved communities in Charlotte. As the foundational partner, Camino Health provides the platform for the delivery of the program, including access to its medical professionals, educational resources, and community network.

UNC Charlotte Sustainable Development Club (Collaborator)

The UNC Charlotte Sustainable Development Club supports the program through volunteer recruitment, fundraising, and awareness campaigns.

Local ESL and Community Organizations (Secondary Partners)

We partner with local ESL programs and community organizations that serve immigrant populations to facilitate the widespread adoption of the health literacy curriculum.

Healthcare Professionals and Faculty Members (Advisory Partners)

Collaborating with healthcare professionals and faculty members ensures the program's content is medically accurate, up-to-date, and relevant.

Community Support Resources

These organizations offer additional support for immigrant communities in Charlotte:
